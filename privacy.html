Privacy Policy

Effective July 3, 2026

The plain-words version: the only personal information this website collects is the email address you choose to give us for the waitlist. We use it to tell you when OllieWise opens. We don't sell it, share it for marketing, or track you around the internet.

Who we are

OllieWise ("we", "us") operates olliewise.com. You can reach us any time at hello@olliewise.com.

What we collect

Waitlist email. If you join the waitlist, we store the email address you submit and the time you submitted it. Providing it is entirely voluntary. We don't ask for your name or anything else.

Basic technical data. Like nearly every website, our infrastructure provider processes standard connection information (such as IP address) to serve pages securely and prevent abuse. We do not run analytics trackers, advertising pixels, or set cookies.

How we use it

Your waitlist email is used for one purpose: to send you a welcome note when you sign up, and to let you know when OllieWise opens. We won't use it for anything else without asking first.

Where it lives, and who helps us

Your email is stored in a database in our own Cloudflare account. We rely on a small number of service providers to run the site: Cloudflare (hosting and storage), Resend (delivering our emails), and Google Fonts (typefaces). These providers process data only as needed to provide their service to us.

What we don't do

We don't sell your information. We don't share it with advertisers. We don't send marketing on behalf of anyone else. This website collects no health information of any kind.

How long we keep it

We keep waitlist emails until OllieWise launches and invitations have gone out, or until you ask us to remove yours — whichever comes first.

Your choices and rights

Want off the list, or curious what we hold about you? Email hello@olliewise.com (or simply reply to any email from us) and we'll remove or share your information promptly. Depending on where you live, you may also have legal rights to access, correct, or delete your personal information — we honor such requests regardless of where you live.

Children

This website is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes

If this policy changes — for example, when the OllieWise app launches — we'll update this page and the date above. Material changes affecting waitlist members will be announced by email.
