Plain words first

You shouldn't need a glossary to understand yourself

Ayurveda has beautiful, precise names for the patterns it describes. We teach them gently, once you've already recognised yourself in plain English. Here's how Ollie might describe a day:

“Running in all directions”

Quick, creative, easily scattered. What helps: grounding, warmth, and rhythm.

Ayurveda calls this pattern Vata.

“Focused and running hot”

Driven, sharp, intense. What helps: cooling off and easing up.

Ayurveda calls this pattern Pitta.

“Steady, but slow to start”

Calm, steady, unhurried. What helps: lightness, movement, and a spark.

Ayurveda calls this pattern Kapha.
