Plain words first

You shouldn't need a glossary to understand yourself

Ayurveda has beautiful, precise names for the patterns it describes. We teach them gently, once you've already recognised yourself in plain English. Here's how Ollie might describe a day:

“Running in all directions”

Quick, creative, easily scattered. Bursts of energy, then a crash. What helps most: grounding, warmth, and rhythm.

Ayurveda calls this pattern Vata — you'll learn the name when you're ready for it.

“Focused and running hot”

Driven, sharp, intense. Strong appetite — for food and for finishing things. What helps most: cooling off and easing up.

Ayurveda calls this pattern Pitta.

“Steady, but slow to start”

Calm, loyal, unhurried. Deep sleep, even keel, mornings are hard. What helps most: lightness, movement, and a spark.

Ayurveda calls this pattern Kapha.
